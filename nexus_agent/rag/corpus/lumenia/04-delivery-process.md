## Lumenia's Four-Step Delivery Approach

Lumenia publishes a four-step delivery process on both the Lumenia homepage (under the heading "Our delivery approach.") and the Lumenia services page. The four steps are numbered 01 through 04: Understand & Plan, Build & Develop, Integrate & Deploy, and Optimize & Scale.

This four-step process is Lumenia's standard engagement flow for AI and software projects. Lumenia's vertical solution pages describe variations on this flow tailored to specific industries, but the four-step approach is the canonical process presented on the Lumenia homepage and services page.

## Step 01 — Understand & Plan

The first step of Lumenia's delivery process is "Understand & Plan".

Lumenia describes this step verbatim as: "We align on your goals, users, and data — defining the right AI strategy and stack to drive real business value."

This step covers goal alignment, understanding end users, understanding the client's data, and selecting the AI strategy and technology stack before any building begins.

## Step 02 — Build & Develop

The second step of Lumenia's delivery process is "Build & Develop".

Lumenia describes this step verbatim as: "We engineer secure, modular AI applications, agents, and automations to production-grade standards."

The emphasis in this step is on security, modularity, and production-grade engineering standards, applied to AI applications, AI agents, and automations.

## Step 03 — Integrate & Deploy

The third step of Lumenia's delivery process is "Integrate & Deploy".

Lumenia describes this step verbatim as: "Smooth deployment of AI-powered systems into your existing tools, data, and workflows."

This step covers deploying the built system and integrating it with the client's existing tools, existing data, and existing workflows rather than replacing them wholesale.

## Step 04 — Optimize & Scale

The fourth step of Lumenia's delivery process is "Optimize & Scale".

Lumenia describes this step verbatim as: "We monitor, fine-tune, and scale — acting as a long-term partner as your product grows."

This step covers post-launch monitoring, fine-tuning, and scaling. Lumenia positions itself here as a long-term partner rather than a one-off vendor. Several Lumenia vertical solution pages describe this ongoing phase as a retainer arrangement.

## Lumenia Process Variations by Vertical

Lumenia's vertical solution pages publish process variations on the same four-phase shape:

- Lumenia's SaaS development process: Audit Call (a 30-minute discovery call), Proposal (scope, timelines, pricing), Deep Build (weekly sprints with staging access), Retainer (ongoing maintenance support).
- Lumenia's ecommerce process: Technical Audit of existing infrastructure, Architecture Blueprint creation, Phased Development with priority features, Ongoing Growth support via retainers.
- Lumenia's accounting firm process: Audit Call to identify inefficiencies, Scoped Proposal with cost and timeline, Build & Deliver with weekly updates, Maintenance retainers.
- Lumenia's mobile app process: discovery calls to scope features, design and prototyping in Figma, iterative building with weekly progress visibility, launch support plus maintenance.
- Lumenia's chatbot process: Use Case Scoping, Bot Design, Build & Integrate, Test & Deploy.
- Lumenia's digital marketing process: Audit & Strategy, Content Planning, Execution, Report & Optimise with monthly performance reports.

A consistent thread across these Lumenia process descriptions is an opening audit or discovery call, a scoped proposal, weekly client visibility during the build, and an ongoing retainer after delivery.
