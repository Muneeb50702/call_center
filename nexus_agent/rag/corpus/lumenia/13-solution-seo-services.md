## Lumenia SEO Overview

Lumenia Lab's SEO services page headline reads: "SEO that compounds — more traffic, every month."

The Lumenia SEO page describes the offering verbatim as: "Technical SEO audits, on-page optimisation, content strategy, and local SEO — by Lumenia Lab."

The Lumenia SEO page states verbatim: "Lumenia Lab provides SEO services for businesses in Pakistan, Canada, the US, UAE, and Qatar."

The primary call to action on the Lumenia SEO page is "Get a Free SEO Audit".

## Lumenia SEO Core Services

Lumenia's SEO services page lists four core services:

- Technical SEO Audit
- On-page Optimisation
- Content Strategy
- Local & International SEO

On technical scope, the Lumenia SEO page states the company fixes technical foundations covering: "canonical URLs, indexation, page speed, Core Web Vitals, structured data, robots.txt, sitemap."

## SEO Problems Lumenia Addresses

Lumenia's SEO services page identifies six ranking obstacles, quoted verbatim:

1. "Stuck on page 2 or beyond"
2. "Zero organic traffic"
3. "Technical issues blocking crawlers"
4. "Thin, low-authority content"
5. "Competitors dominating local search"
6. "No structured data"

## Lumenia SEO Timeline Expectation

On how long SEO results take, the Lumenia SEO services page states verbatim: "Most businesses see meaningful organic traffic growth within 3 to 6 months."

This 3-to-6-month window is the only timeline commitment published anywhere on the Lumenia website. Lumenia does not publish delivery timelines for its software, SaaS, mobile, or AI engagements — those pages direct prospects to a scoped proposal following an audit or discovery call.

Note: Lumenia's SEO services page does not publish pricing, named SEO case studies, or ranking metrics from past SEO work.
