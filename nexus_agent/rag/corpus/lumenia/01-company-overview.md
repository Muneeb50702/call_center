## What Lumenia Is

Lumenia (also referred to on its website as Lumenia Lab) is an AI-driven software company. The Lumenia website describes the company as "An AI-driven software company building AI-powered applications, autonomous agents, and automation — production-grade systems for businesses worldwide."

The Lumenia homepage headline is "We build AI software that ships." The supporting statement reads: "From AI-powered applications and autonomous agents to automation and custom platforms — Lumenia delivers production-grade AI software with complete ownership."

Lumenia is headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan. The site states Lumenia is "Serving clients in CA · US · AE · QA" — that is, Canada, the United States, the United Arab Emirates, and Qatar.

The primary contact for Lumenia is umair@lumenialab.com, phone +92 335 4455494.

## Lumenia Site Structure

The Lumenia website is organized into the following main sections: Home, Services (with six individual service pages), Work/Projects (an index of 18 projects), Blog, About, and Contact. Lumenia also publishes vertical solution pages covering SaaS development, ecommerce, accounting firms, mobile app development, chatbot development, digital marketing, and SEO services. Legal pages cover Terms and Privacy.

The primary calls to action across the Lumenia site are "Book a Consultation", "View Our Work", and "Get a Free Estimate", all pointing to the Lumenia contact page.

The Lumenia site footer carries the notice "© 2026 Lumenia · All rights reserved" and repeats "Serving clients in CA · US · AE · QA".
