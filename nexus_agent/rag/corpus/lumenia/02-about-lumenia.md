## How Lumenia Describes Itself

The Lumenia about page states verbatim: "Lumenia Lab is a lean, founder-led AI software company."

The Lumenia about page states the company's core belief as: "Businesses deserve software that actually works in production."

Lumenia is headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan, and the about page repeats the markets line "Serving clients in CA · US · AE · QA" (Canada, United States, United Arab Emirates, Qatar).

Contact details listed on the Lumenia about page are umair@lumenialab.com and +92 335 4455494.

## Lumenia Track Record

The Lumenia about page states verbatim: "50+ production systems — AI-powered applications, agents, automation, SaaS platforms, and custom software — for clients across four continents."

This is the fullest statement of Lumenia's track record on the site. The Lumenia homepage renders the same claim more briefly as "50+ Systems shipped" and "100% Completion rate".

Note: the Lumenia website does not state a team size, a founding date, or a client roster beyond the projects published in the Lumenia work section and the named brands within them.

## Lumenia Core Service Areas

The Lumenia about page groups the company's work into four areas:

- Autonomous AI Agents
- Custom SaaS & Web Platforms
- Backend & Systems Engineering
- Integration & Cloud Infrastructure

These four about-page areas are a summary view. Lumenia's services section presents six formal service lines: AI-Powered Applications, AI Development & Integration, AI Agentic Automation, AI Consulting & Strategy, AI SaaS Platforms, and Domain & Hosting Automation.

## Lumenia Technology Stack

The Lumenia about page names the following technologies: Next.js, React, Node.js, serverless backends, AWS, and Vercel.

The Lumenia about page also names these specialized capabilities: multi-agent LLM systems, custom RAG pipelines, automated business workflows, REST/GraphQL APIs, background job queues, custom webhooks, and CRM syncing.

Note on stack: while the Lumenia about page emphasizes Next.js, React, Node.js, AWS and Vercel, the majority of the individual project pages in Lumenia's work section list Laravel, PHP, and MySQL as the delivered stack. Several Lumenia project pages describe this as the "TALL Stack", listing its components as Tailwind CSS, Laravel, MySQL, HTML, PHP and Alpine.js.

## Lumenia Stated Differentiators

The Lumenia about page lists the following key differentiators:

- Complete ownership of the full lifecycle
- Production systems, not prototypes
- Direct engineer communication
- Honest engineering with transparent pricing

The "direct engineer communication" and "founder-led" points align with the Lumenia contact page, which identifies Umair Tufail as Founder & Lead Engineer and the direct point of contact for enquiries.
