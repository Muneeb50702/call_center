## Lumenia Privacy Policy Overview

Lumenia Lab publishes a Privacy Policy at https://lumenialab.com/privacy with an effective date of June 2025.

The Lumenia privacy policy states verbatim: "Your information is used only for the purposes listed below. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described here."

The contact address for privacy matters at Lumenia is umair@lumenialab.com.

## Data Lumenia Collects

The Lumenia privacy policy states the company collects:

- Contact form submissions — name, email, message
- Newsletter subscriptions — email address
- Client project details — name, company, billing address, payment info

## Lumenia Third Parties and Data Processors

The Lumenia privacy policy names the following third parties:

- **Supabase** — stores contact and newsletter data on encrypted cloud infrastructure.
- **Payment processors** — bank transfer and Wise handle transactions. The Lumenia privacy policy states these do not store full banking details on Lumenia's systems.

Wise and bank transfer are the only payment methods Lumenia names for receiving client payments.

## Lumenia Cookies and Analytics

The Lumenia privacy policy states the company uses minimal, privacy-respecting analytics that track general traffic patterns only.

The policy states verbatim: "We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or any form of cross-site tracking."

## Lumenia Data Retention and User Rights

Retention periods stated in the Lumenia privacy policy:

- Contact submissions — retained up to 3 years from last contact
- Client records — retained 5 years for accounting and legal compliance

User rights stated in the Lumenia privacy policy: access, correction, deletion, and newsletter unsubscribe, all available on request and actioned within 30 days.
