## What does Lumenia do?

**Q: What does Lumenia do?**

A: Lumenia (Lumenia Lab) is an AI-driven software company that builds AI-powered applications, autonomous agents, and automation. Lumenia's own description reads: "An AI-driven software company building AI-powered applications, autonomous agents, and automation — production-grade systems for businesses worldwide." The Lumenia homepage headline is "We build AI software that ships."

**Q: What kind of company is Lumenia?**

A: Lumenia's about page describes it verbatim as "a lean, founder-led AI software company." Lumenia's stated belief is that "Businesses deserve software that actually works in production." Lumenia emphasizes shipping production-grade systems rather than prototypes, and handing clients complete ownership of the result.

**Q: What services does Lumenia offer?**

A: Lumenia offers six service lines: AI-Powered Applications; AI Development & Integration; AI Agentic Automation; AI Consulting & Strategy; AI SaaS Platforms; and Domain & Hosting Automation. Lumenia also publishes solution pages for SaaS development, custom ecommerce, software for accounting and tax firms, mobile app development, chatbot development, digital marketing, and SEO services.

## Where is Lumenia located and who do they serve?

**Q: Where is Lumenia located?**

A: Lumenia is headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan, in the UTC+5 timezone.

**Q: What markets does Lumenia serve?**

A: Lumenia's homepage, about page, and contact page all state "Serving clients in CA · US · AE · QA" — Canada, the United States, the United Arab Emirates, and Qatar. Lumenia's about page states the company has delivered "for clients across four continents."

Note that some Lumenia solution pages list different market sets: the SaaS page says CA, US, UAE and AU; the ecommerce page says UAE, US and AU; the accounting firms page says Australia and the US; and the chatbot, digital marketing and SEO pages say Canada, US, UAE, Qatar and Pakistan. Lumenia has also delivered at least one project in London, UK (Mobile Valeting London for Splash and Drip).

**Q: Who is the contact at Lumenia?**

A: Umair Tufail, identified on the Lumenia contact page as Founder & Lead Engineer.

## What does Lumenia cost?

**Q: How much does Lumenia charge?**

A: Lumenia does not publish rates, package prices, or fixed project prices on its website. Lumenia's SaaS page states: "For custom project pricing and detailed technical breakdown, please click below to start a conversation." Lumenia's about page describes its approach as "Honest engineering with transparent pricing", and each service page offers "an honest estimate within 24 hours."

**Q: What budget ranges does Lumenia work with?**

A: The budget dropdown on Lumenia's contact form offers: "< $2k", "$2k – $5k", "$5k – $15k", "$15k+", and "Not sure yet". These are the only figures resembling price points on the Lumenia website.

**Q: What are Lumenia's payment terms?**

A: Lumenia's terms page states a deposit of 40-50% is due before work starts, with the remainder at agreed milestones or on delivery. Invoices are payable within the number of days stated on the invoice, work pauses if payment is overdue beyond 7 days, and fees are non-refundable once work on the relevant milestone has commenced. Lumenia's privacy policy names bank transfer and Wise as its payment methods.

## What is Lumenia's development process?

**Q: What is Lumenia's development process?**

A: Lumenia publishes a four-step delivery approach:

1. **Understand & Plan** — "We align on your goals, users, and data — defining the right AI strategy and stack to drive real business value."
2. **Build & Develop** — "We engineer secure, modular AI applications, agents, and automations to production-grade standards."
3. **Integrate & Deploy** — "Smooth deployment of AI-powered systems into your existing tools, data, and workflows."
4. **Optimize & Scale** — "We monitor, fine-tune, and scale — acting as a long-term partner as your product grows."

**Q: What does a Lumenia engagement look like in practice?**

A: Lumenia's vertical pages describe the same shape with more detail: an opening audit or discovery call (30 minutes on the SaaS page), a scoped proposal covering scope, timelines and pricing, a build phase with weekly sprints and staging access or weekly updates, and an ongoing retainer for maintenance after delivery.

**Q: Who owns the code Lumenia writes?**

A: Lumenia's terms state that upon full payment, clients receive complete ownership of final deliverables including source code and design files. Lumenia retains ownership of its pre-existing general-purpose tools, libraries and code patterns, licensed to clients royalty-free.

## What technologies does Lumenia use?

**Q: What is Lumenia's technology stack?**

A: Lumenia's about page names Next.js, React, Node.js, serverless backends, AWS, and Vercel, plus multi-agent LLM systems, custom RAG pipelines, automated business workflows, REST/GraphQL APIs, background job queues, custom webhooks, and CRM syncing. Most of Lumenia's individual published project pages list Laravel, PHP and MySQL, often as part of what Lumenia calls the TALL Stack (Tailwind CSS, Laravel, MySQL, HTML, PHP, Alpine.js). Lumenia's blog post on payment and accounting APIs states: "Stripe, Square, QuickBooks, crypto gateways — we've shipped them all."

**Q: Does Lumenia build chatbots?**

A: Yes. Lumenia's chatbot page headline is "AI chatbots that handle support, sales & ops." Lumenia builds WhatsApp chatbots, website AI assistants trained on client documentation and FAQs, RAG-powered knowledge bots, and Telegram & Slack bots for internal operations.

## What Lumenia does not publish

**Q: Are there things the Lumenia website does not state?**

A: Yes. Prospective claims that the Lumenia website does not support include: team size or headcount; company founding date or years in business; project delivery timelines for software engagements (only the SEO page gives a timeline — "Most businesses see meaningful organic traffic growth within 3 to 6 months"); named client references beyond the brands appearing in project titles and live URLs; hourly rates or fixed prices; quantified performance results for individual projects other than the "50k+ products" figure on the Hakkidd ecommerce dashboard; and any service-level agreement or uptime guarantee.

**Q: Which Lumenia projects are named but have no detail page?**

A: Two projects are named on Lumenia's solution pages but have no dedicated project page in the Lumenia work index: "911 Limo App" (named on the SaaS page as ride-sharing with live tracking) and "Construction AI" (named on the accounting firms page as a workflow SaaS). "Ezanias TMS" is named on three Lumenia pages as Canadian logistics SaaS but does not appear under that name in the Lumenia project index, which publishes a "SaaS Trucking Management & Dispatch System" for Canadian trucking enterprises.
