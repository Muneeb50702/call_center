## Lumenia Chatbot Development Overview

Lumenia's chatbot development page headline reads: "AI chatbots that handle support, sales & ops."

Lumenia specializes in WhatsApp bots, website AI assistants, RAG-powered knowledge bots, and Telegram integrations designed to automate business operations.

The Lumenia chatbot page states the company serves clients in Canada, US, UAE, Qatar, and Pakistan.

Lumenia's chatbot page calls to action are "Get a Free Chatbot Consultation" and "Build My Chatbot".

## Types of Chatbots Lumenia Builds

Lumenia's chatbot development page lists four types of bot:

- **WhatsApp Chatbots** — for automated support and lead qualification
- **Website AI Assistants** — trained on the client's documentation and FAQs
- **RAG-Powered Knowledge Bots** — using retrieval-augmented generation
- **Telegram & Slack Bots** — for internal operations

Core capabilities Lumenia lists for chatbot work: WhatsApp Business API integration, RAG-powered bots, LLM integration, and 24/7 automation coverage.

Lumenia's about page separately confirms the company builds custom RAG pipelines and multi-agent LLM systems.

## Chatbot Problems Lumenia Addresses

Lumenia's chatbot development page identifies six pain points:

1. Slow support response times
2. Staff handling repetitive queries
3. No 24/7 coverage for leads
4. Disconnected systems lacking customer data access
5. Rule-based bots with poor user experience
6. Inability to escalate to human agents
