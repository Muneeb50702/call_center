## Lumenia Accounting Firm Solutions Overview

Lumenia's accounting firms solution page headline reads: "Your team is losing 10+ hours a week to admin."

Lumenia offers custom workflow tools, client portals, and automation systems designed specifically for accounting, audit, and tax firms. The Lumenia accounting firms page states these are aimed at firms across Australia and the US.

Note on markets: the Lumenia accounting firms page names Australia and the US, whereas Lumenia's homepage and about page state markets as "CA · US · AE · QA". Both statements appear on the Lumenia website.

## Lumenia Firm-Specific Solutions

Lumenia's accounting firms page lists four firm-specific solutions:

- **Client Document Portal** — with secure uploads, audit trails, and role-based access
- **Deadline & Task System** — with automated reminders and staff assignment
- **Workflow Automation** — including onboarding flows and document requests
- **Billing & Invoicing Dashboard** — with time tracking and automated invoice generation

The Lumenia accounting firms page also presents the company's six core service lines: AI-Powered Applications, AI Development & Integration, AI Agentic Automation, AI Consulting & Strategy, AI SaaS Platforms, and Domain & Hosting Automation.

## Accounting Firm Problems Lumenia Addresses

Lumenia's accounting firms page identifies six problems:

- **Document Chaos** — insecure email sharing of client documents
- **Missed Deadlines** — lack of centralized deadline tracking
- **Scattered Files** — fragmented storage across systems
- **Staff Visibility Gaps** — no real-time project status
- **Manual Billing** — time-consuming invoice generation
- **Client Entity Chaos** — difficulty managing multiple client companies

## Lumenia Accounting Firm Engagement Process

Lumenia's accounting firms page publishes a four-step process:

1. **Audit Call** to identify inefficiencies
2. **Scoped Proposal** with cost and timeline
3. **Build & Deliver** with weekly updates
4. **Maintenance** retainers after delivery
