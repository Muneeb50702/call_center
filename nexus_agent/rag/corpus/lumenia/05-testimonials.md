## Lumenia Testimonials Overview

The Lumenia homepage publishes three client testimonials under the section heading "Trusted worldwide." Each testimonial is attributed to a role and an industry rather than to a named individual or a named company. Lumenia does not publish named client references, photographs, or full company attributions alongside these quotes.

The three Lumenia testimonials cover a trucking/logistics SaaS client in Canada, a FoodTech client in Calgary, and an AI HR Tech client described as global. All three testimonials are reproduced verbatim in the sections below.

## Lumenia Testimonial — Logistics and Dispatch

The following testimonial appears on the Lumenia homepage, quoted verbatim:

"Lumenia rebuilt our dispatch system from scratch and integrated QuickBooks flawlessly. Our invoicing is fully automated now."

Attribution as published by Lumenia: Logistics Founder, Trucking SaaS · Canada.

This testimonial corresponds to the domain of Lumenia's SaaS Trucking Management & Dispatch System project, which lists QuickBooks API integration among the features Lumenia built.

## Lumenia Testimonial — Food Delivery Platform

The following testimonial appears on the Lumenia homepage, quoted verbatim:

"They delivered our food delivery platform end-to-end — storefront, admin, and Deliverect integration. Production-grade from day one."

Attribution as published by Lumenia: Restaurant Owner, FoodTech · Calgary.

This testimonial corresponds to the domain of Lumenia's Medaan project, a food delivery and brand experience platform for the Canadian market which lists Deliverect API integration among the features Lumenia built.

## Lumenia Testimonial — AI Resume Engine

The following testimonial appears on the Lumenia homepage, quoted verbatim:

"The AI resume engine they built parses and tailors CVs better than anything we tested. Our application success rate jumped immediately."

Attribution as published by Lumenia: Founder, AI HR Tech · Global.

This testimonial corresponds to the domain of Lumenia's CareerOS project, an AI-powered job application lifecycle platform which lists AI-driven resume parsing and generation among the features Lumenia built. Lumenia does not publish a specific percentage or figure for the "application success rate" increase referenced in this quote.
