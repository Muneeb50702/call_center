## Lumenia Digital Marketing Overview

Lumenia Lab's digital marketing page describes the company as a "digital marketing agency" providing social media management, Google Ads, content marketing, and email campaigns.

The Lumenia digital marketing page states the company serves clients across Canada, the US, UAE, Qatar, and Pakistan.

The primary call to action on the Lumenia digital marketing page is "Get a Free Marketing Audit". The page carries the note: "No long-term contracts required."

Digital marketing sits alongside Lumenia's core AI software engineering service lines and its SEO services offering.

## Lumenia Digital Marketing Services

Lumenia's digital marketing page lists four services:

- **Social Media Management** — content creation, scheduling, and growth strategy across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook
- **Google Ads & PPC** — search, display, and shopping campaigns optimized for conversions
- **Content Marketing** — SEO-driven blog content and thought leadership pieces
- **Email Campaigns** — drip sequences, newsletters, and lifecycle campaigns

## Marketing Problems Lumenia Addresses

Lumenia's digital marketing page identifies six marketing pain points:

- Lack of website traffic
- Poor ad ROI
- Inconsistent brand presence
- Competitors outranking the client
- Missing lead generation systems
- Poor marketing visibility

## Lumenia Digital Marketing Process

Lumenia's digital marketing page publishes a four-step approach:

1. **Audit & Strategy**
2. **Content Planning**
3. **Execution**
4. **Report & Optimise** — with monthly performance reports

Note: Lumenia's digital marketing page does not publish pricing, retainer fees, named marketing case studies, or performance metrics from past marketing campaigns.
