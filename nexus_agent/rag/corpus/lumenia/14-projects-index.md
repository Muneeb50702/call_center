## Lumenia Portfolio Overview

The Lumenia work portfolio index describes the company's projects verbatim as: "Custom web apps, mobile apps, SaaS platforms, and AI systems — engineered from the ground up."

Lumenia publishes 18 projects in its work section at https://lumenialab.com/projects. The Lumenia homepage separately claims "50+ Systems shipped" and the Lumenia about page claims "50+ production systems ... for clients across four continents", so the 18 published projects are a showcase subset rather than the company's full delivery history.

Lumenia's role is listed as "Full-Stack Engineering" on every one of the 18 project pages. The Lumenia project index does not display filter categories.
