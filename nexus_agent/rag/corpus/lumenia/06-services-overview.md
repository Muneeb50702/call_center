## Lumenia Services Overview

The Lumenia services page headline reads: "Web development, app development & custom software that scales."

The Lumenia services page intro reads verbatim: "From custom web applications and mobile apps to AI-powered SaaS platforms and autonomous agents — full-stack software development, shipped production-grade."

Lumenia offers six formal service lines:

1. AI-Powered Applications
2. AI Development & Integration
3. AI Agentic Automation
4. AI Consulting & Strategy
5. AI SaaS Platforms
6. Domain & Hosting Automation

The Lumenia homepage frames these services with the line "AI services that deliver." and "From strategy to shipped systems — every engagement is production-grade, not a prototype."

Lumenia does not publish pricing on its services pages. Each Lumenia service page carries the call to action: "Tell us about your project and we'll reply with an honest estimate within 24 hours."
