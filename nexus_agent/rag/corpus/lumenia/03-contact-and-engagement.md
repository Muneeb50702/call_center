## Lumenia Engagement and Payment Model

Lumenia's published engagement model is drawn from the Lumenia terms page and vertical solution pages.

Payment terms stated on the Lumenia terms page: a deposit of 40-50% is due before work starts, with the remainder due at agreed milestones or on delivery. Invoices are "payable within the number of days stated on the invoice". Work pauses if payment is overdue beyond 7 days. "Fees are non-refundable once work on the relevant milestone has commenced."

Several Lumenia vertical pages describe engagements ending in an ongoing retainer for maintenance and support. The Lumenia digital marketing page states "No long-term contracts required."

Lumenia describes its pricing approach on the about page as "Honest engineering with transparent pricing", and offers "an honest estimate within 24 hours" on each service page.
