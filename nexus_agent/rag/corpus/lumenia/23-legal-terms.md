## Lumenia Terms Overview

Lumenia Lab publishes Terms & Conditions at https://lumenialab.com/terms with an effective date of June 2025. The company legal name given is Lumenia Lab.

The Lumenia terms state the agreement is "governed by the laws of Pakistan" with "exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Lahore, Pakistan".

These terms are the most detailed statement of Lumenia's commercial model published on the website, covering payment structure, intellectual property handover, warranties, and liability limits.

## Lumenia Payment Terms

The Lumenia terms page states the following payment structure:

- A deposit of 40-50% is due before work starts.
- The remainder is due at agreed milestones or on delivery.
- Invoices are "payable within the number of days stated on the invoice".
- Work pauses if payment is overdue beyond 7 days.
- "Fees are non-refundable once work on the relevant milestone has commenced."

This 40-50% upfront deposit is the only concrete commercial term Lumenia publishes. Lumenia does not publish rates, package prices, or fixed project prices anywhere on its website. The budget bands on the Lumenia contact form ("< $2k", "$2k – $5k", "$5k – $15k", "$15k+", "Not sure yet") indicate the ranges Lumenia asks prospects to self-select.

## Lumenia Intellectual Property and Ownership

The Lumenia terms page states that upon full payment, clients receive complete ownership of final deliverables including source code and design files.

Lumenia Lab retains ownership of its pre-existing "general-purpose tools, libraries, or code patterns", which are licensed to clients royalty-free.

This ownership position matches the claim on Lumenia's homepage that the company delivers "production-grade AI software with complete ownership" and the about-page differentiator of "Complete ownership of full lifecycle".

## Lumenia Warranties and Liability

The Lumenia terms page states that work is delivered "with reasonable skill and care, and will materially conform to the agreed specification."

Lumenia does not guarantee bug-free software indefinitely. Post-delivery defects are addressed during support periods at no extra cost.

On liability, the Lumenia terms state: "total liability...is limited to the total fees paid by you for that project." Indirect, consequential, and special losses are excluded.
