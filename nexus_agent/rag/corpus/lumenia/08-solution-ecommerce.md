## Lumenia Ecommerce Overview

Lumenia's ecommerce solution page headline reads: "Your store is growing. Your backend should keep up."

Lumenia provides custom order management, inventory systems, and admin dashboards for ecommerce brands that have outgrown standard platforms.

Capabilities Lumenia lists on its ecommerce page include payment gateway integrations, custom admin dashboards, and experience handling 10k+ orders. The Lumenia ecommerce page states the company serves UAE, US, and AU clients.

Note on markets: the Lumenia ecommerce page lists UAE, US and AU (Australia), whereas Lumenia's homepage and about page state markets as "CA · US · AE · QA". Both statements appear on the Lumenia website.

## Ecommerce Problems Lumenia Addresses

Lumenia's ecommerce page identifies six pain points affecting growing stores:

- Slow admin panels
- Inventory fragmentation across warehouses
- Manual order processing
- Unsupported payment gateways
- Inadequate analytics
- Disconnected business systems

Lumenia positions its ecommerce work at brands that have outgrown off-the-shelf platforms and need custom backend systems rather than storefront theming.

## Lumenia Ecommerce Solutions

Lumenia's ecommerce page lists four solutions:

- **Custom Order Management** systems
- **Inventory & Warehouse Backend** supporting multiple warehouses
- **Bespoke Admin Dashboard** portals
- **Payment and API Integrations**

Across Lumenia's individual ecommerce project pages, the payment and fulfilment integrations Lumenia names as delivered include Square API, Deliverect API, ShipStation API, Mixpanel API, 3D-secure payment gateways, and barcode APIs.

## Lumenia Ecommerce Process

Lumenia's ecommerce page publishes a four-phase process:

1. **Technical Audit** of existing infrastructure
2. **Architecture Blueprint** creation
3. **Phased Development** with priority features first
4. **Ongoing Growth** support via retainers

## Lumenia Ecommerce Case Studies

Lumenia's ecommerce page references five of the company's projects:

- **Medaan** — food delivery platform for the Canadian market
- **Bali Jewels** — jewelry retail ecommerce for the Canadian market
- **ANR Inventory** — ERP and supply chain management
- **Balloon Dubai** — booking platform for Dubai adventure services, published in Lumenia's work index as "Hot Air Balloon & Tour Booking Website"
- **Vendor Shendor** — multi-vendor marketplace

Each of these five projects has a dedicated project page in Lumenia's work section.
